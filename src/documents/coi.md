# Conflict of Interest Declaration

I, **{{LEGAL_NAME}}**, hereby declare that I understand and acknowledge the following:

## 1. Purpose

This Conflict of Interest Declaration is designed to protect the interests of MYSverse Digital Ventures and its team members by ensuring transparency regarding any external commitments, relationships, or activities that could potentially conflict with my responsibilities.

## 2. Obligations

As a member of the MYSverse Digital Ventures team, I agree to:

- Disclose any current or future business relationships, employment, or consulting arrangements with organizations that compete with or are relevant to MYSverse Digital Ventures's operations.
- Promptly update this declaration if my circumstances change.
- Act in the best interest of MYSverse Digital Ventures and avoid situations where my personal interests could conflict with my professional duties.
- Refrain from using confidential information obtained through MYSverse Digital Ventures for personal benefit or for the benefit of any competing organization.

## 3. Competing Commitments

I understand that I must declare all competing commitments, involvements with competitors, and any other activities that may present a conflict of interest. These declarations can be updated at any time through the MYSverse Digital Ventures dashboard.

## 4. Consequences

I understand that failure to disclose a conflict of interest, or providing false information in this declaration, may result in disciplinary action, including termination of my engagement with MYSverse Digital Ventures.

## 5. Acknowledgment

By signing this declaration, I confirm that:

- I have read and understood this Conflict of Interest Declaration.
- I will comply with the obligations outlined above.
- I will promptly disclose any changes to my circumstances that may create a conflict of interest.
